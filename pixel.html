Pixel 7 Pro

Google Pixel 7 Pro mobile was launched on 6th October 2022. The phone comes with a 120 Hz refresh rate 6.70-inch touchscreen display offering a resolution of 1440x3120 pixels (QHD). Google Pixel 7 Pro is based on Android 13 and packs 256GB of inbuilt storage

Google Pixel 7 Pro 5G packs a massive RAM of 12GB backed by Mali-G78 MP20 GPU and a Google Tensor G2 chipset. Its 64-bit Octa-Core processor stands with Cortex X1, Cortex A76 and Cortex A55 layout, clocking at a speed of 2.8GHz, 2.25GHz and 1.8GHz precisely.

Connectivity options on the Google Pixel 7 Pro include Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/ax, GPS, and USB Type-C with active 4G on both SIM cards. Sensors on the phone include accelerometer, ambient light sensor, compass/ magnetometer, gyroscope, proximity sensor, and in-display fingerprint sensor. The Google Pixel 7 Pro supports face unlock.
